Hello!

Welcome to Idottone, a simple music pad made with ToneJs.

Usage

You can either use your AZERTY keyboard or click on the notes.
Please note that you need to switch to an AZERTY keyboard to play.

Record/Play/Copy

Record permits you to record 10 seconds of your following sequence of playing.
Play permits you to replay what others sent to you or your own record.
Copy copies a link that stores your last record. That way, you can share it to your friends!

Lyre/Harp

Lyre switches to the Genshin Impact Windsong Lyre (Thanks to p_izzadad on Reddit for the sample library! ♥)
LyreV2 switches to a more sustained Genshin Impact Windsong Lyre (Thanks to Specy for the sample library! ♥)
Harp switches to a harp from samples of this repo on Github.
